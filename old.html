About Don Howard Berries

I'm Don Howard Berries, and I've been growing the berries since 2006.

What started as a small patch in my backyard has blossomed into a thriving family business!

My secret? It's simple: sunshine, clean water, and my daily practice of breathwork and meditation. Every berry is handpicked by me at the peak of ripeness.

I personally inspect each batch before it leaves our farm. That's the DON HOWARD BERRIES GUARANTEE.
