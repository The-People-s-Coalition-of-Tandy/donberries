My Story

Since 2006, I, Don Howard Berries, have dedicated my life to growing the finest berries in the country. What started as a small patch in my backyard has blossomed into a thriving family business.

Our secret? It's simple: sunshine, clean water, and my daily meditation and breathwork practice. Every berry is handpicked at the peak of ripeness to ensure maximum flavor and nutritional value.

I personally inspected each batch before it leaves our farm. That's the Don Howard Berries guarantee - berries so good, they'll change your life!

As I always say, "Life is better with berries!" And once you've tried Don Howard Berries, you'll never go back to ordinary fruit again.
